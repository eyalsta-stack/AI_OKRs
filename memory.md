# OKR Tree — Design to Agile Execution Pain Points

> 14 objectives mapped to key results and actionable solutions for enterprise communications

**Legend:** 🔵 Objective · 🟡 Key Result · 🟢 Solution

---

## 1. Sprint planning & scoping

### Objective 1: Break down stories faster

**Key Result:** Decrease time to sign-off time to sprint-ready stories

| # | Solution | Personas |
|---|----------|----------|
| S1 | Standard story-splitting template | PM, Tech Lead |
| S2 | Design + dev breakdown workshop (meeting) | PM, Design, Dev |
| S3 | AI generated stories from design specs | PM, Design |
| S4 | AI auto-splits epics into stories from design files | PM, Design |

---

### Objective 2: Match dev output to design intent

**Key Result:** 60% fewer design-rework tickets per quarter

| # | Solution | Personas |
|---|----------|----------|
| S1 | Handoff specs with edge-case notes | Design, Dev |
| S2 | Shared checklist before sprint entry | Design, Dev, PM |
| S3 | Mid-sprint design or demo check-ins | Design, Dev |
| S4 | AI matching between deliverable and design spec and flag mismatches | Dev, Design, QA |

---

### Objective 3: Write clear acceptance criteria

**Key Result:** 100% of stories have QA-reviewed testable AC

| # | Solution | Personas |
|---|----------|----------|
| S1 | QA signs off AC in refinement | QA, PM |
| S2 | AI drafts AC from story description + design spec | PM, QA |

---

### Objective 4: Speed up refinement meetings

**Key Result:** Improve Refinement/Grooming meetings efficiency

| # | Solution | Personas |
|---|----------|----------|
| S1 | Clear agenda and review of task by all team members before the meeting | PM, Dev, QA |
| S2 | AI flagging of potential problems before refinement | PM, Tech Lead |
